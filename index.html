About Us

Founded in 2022, Chaos Hunters Esports was born from a shared passion for competitive gaming. Our goal is to create an esports organization where both boys and girls can showcase their skills, compete with the best, and inspire the next generation of gamers.

Vision: To become one of the most respected and inclusive esports organizations.

Mission:

  • Build rosters for Valorant, Free Fire, and BGMI
  • Provide opportunities for talented players of all genders
